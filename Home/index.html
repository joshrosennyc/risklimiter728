Our History

A History of Prevention.

The software solutions originally launched as ESP (Electronic Slip/Fall Prevention) and CCPi (Critical Control Point Inspections), designed to address the growing challenges of rising insurance premiums, general liability & workers' compensation claims, and food safety and compliance issues.

As the proprietary platform evolved—expanding its inspection capabilities and technological sophistication—it was rebranded as RiskLimiter to highlight its core value: reducing risk, ensuring compliance, and lowering operational costs.
