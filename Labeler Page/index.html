Cloud-Based Product List

RiskLimiter Labeler uses a cloud-based product list to ensure the accurate printing of preparation labels. The product name, shelf life, PLU, and product category can be centrally managed from our online Dashboard.

Bulk and Individual Printing

Preparing more than one item? Set the count of labels needed directly in the app and watch RiskLimiter Labeler print at speeds upward of 5 labels per second. Save significant labor costs on the time-consuming prep label task by printing multiple labels at a time.

Data Tracking and Expiration Reporting

From a centralized dashboard monitor the print history and expiration date of every product in-house. When a product is set to expire, RiskLimiter Labeler will automatically flag the product for removal.
